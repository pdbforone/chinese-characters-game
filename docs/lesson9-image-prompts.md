## Act I: The Journey (Distance & Measurement)

### 1. 里 (lǐ) - a Li (Tone 3: GROAN)

```

The figure pauses where rice paddies meet bare earth, a bamboo counting staff in hand. Geometric field plots stretch to the horizon in horizontal layers, each one representing a li walked. The packed soil path continues into mountain shadows. Exhaustion and determination war on the traveler's face.

The composition arranges terraced fields above packed earth to subtly evoke the Chinese character 里, as if generations of travelers wearing this pattern of distance into the land.
```

## Usage Notes

### Tone-Visual Energy Mapping

| Tone        | Emotion           | Visual Energy                  | Characters                                 |
| ----------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| 1 (SING)    | Sustained, serene | Balanced, horizontal, peaceful | 黑, 宣, 宵, 安                             |
| 2 (GASP)    | Rising surprise   | Upward motion, lifting         | 埋, 同, 完, 實                             |
| 3 (GROAN)   | Heavy, weighted   | Downward pressure, grounded    | 里, 點, 守                                 |
| 4 (COMMAND) | Sharp, decisive   | Decisive motion, angular       | 量, 墨, 冒, 洞, 向, 尚, 字, 宴, 寄, 富, 貯 |

### Color Palette for Lesson 9

Given the "Black Ink Banquet" theme, the palette should emphasize:

- **Primary:** Lamp-black ink in multiple gradations
- **Secondary:** Cinnabar red (torch-light, fire)
- **Accent:** Azurite blue (water in caves)
- **Rare:** Malachite green (for journey/field scenes only)
- **Highlights:** Mineral white (faces, paper, spots of light)
