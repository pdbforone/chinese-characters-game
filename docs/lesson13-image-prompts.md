## Image Generation Notes

### Color Palette

- **Roads/Way**: Earth ochres, stone grays
- **Lotus**: Pink petals, green stems
- **Cars/Carts**: Warm wood browns
- **Summer heat**: Yellows, oranges, heat shimmer
- **Water**: Azurite blues
- **Sweat/Effort**: Gleaming highlights
