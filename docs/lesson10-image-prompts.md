## Usage Notes

| Tone        | Emotion           | Visual Energy                  | Characters                                         |
| ----------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |

### Color Palette for Lesson 10

Given the "Forest of Dim-Witted Trees" theme:

- **Primary:** Malachite greens (forest, leaves, grass, seedling)
- **Secondary:** Warm ochres and browns (wood, village, earth)
- **Accent:** Soft apricot/peach tones (fruit, flowers)
- **Contrast:** Pale yellows and desert sands (withering, emptiness)
- **Hope:** Fresh spring green (seedling at the end)

### Narrative Arc for Image Generation

Generate images in sequence to tell the story:

1. **Opening:** Single tree → woods → forest (木→林→森)
2. **Comedy:** Stupid apricots, dim-wittedness, withering (杏→呆→枯)
3. **Community:** Village at edge, mutual dependence (村→相)
4. **Mystery:** Investigation begins (本→案→未)
5. **Tragedy:** Death and absurd mourning (末→沫→味)
6. **Quest:** Sister investigates (妹→查→渣→染→李)
7. **Abandonment:** Empty house, nature reclaims (桌→若→草→苦)
8. **Vastness:** Endless forest, unanswered questions (寬→葉→莫→模)
9. **Endings:** Desert, grave, hope (漠→墓→苗→瞄)
